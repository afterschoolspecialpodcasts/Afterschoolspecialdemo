Frequently Asked

How do I get my topic on the show?

Fill out the form on this page with as much detail as you can. The more context you give — why the topic matters, what angles you'd want covered, specific memories tied to it — the better chance it makes it into an episode.

Can I be a guest on the podcast?

Guest inquiries are welcome. If you've got a story, expertise, or a connection to throwback Detroit culture that feels right for the show, mention it in your message and the crew will follow up.

Where are new episodes released?

New episodes drop on Spotify first, followed by Apple Podcasts and YouTube. Follow on any platform to get notified automatically, or check the Episodes page for the latest.
