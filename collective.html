What We’re Building

More Than Just One Show

After-School with Friends started as one podcast and turned into something bigger. Today it’s a growing collective of independent voices — covering throwback culture, sports and pop culture, mental health, and everything in between. Different shows. Same family. One mission: keep the conversation real, and keep it moving.
