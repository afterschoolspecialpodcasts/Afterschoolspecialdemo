Episodes 107 – 103

Each panel links directly to its episode on Spotify. Hit play inside the embed or tap the card to open in the app.

EP. 107

Bay City Secrets: Michigan History & The Oakland County Child Killer Connection

The crew pulls back the curtain on one of Michigan's darkest unsolved cases, tracing threads connecting small-town Bay City to the infamous Oakland County Child Killer.

EP. 106

Unraveling The Fresh Prince of Bel-Air

The crew goes deep on the cultural DNA of The Fresh Prince — the fashion, the family dynamics, and why this show hit different if you grew up in a city like Detroit.

EP. 105

(Bonus) Retro Rewind

A rapid-fire throwback bonus episode packed with forgotten TV shows, discontinued snacks, and the slang that never should have died — from the Detroit archives.

EP. 104

Music Videos and Beyond

Before YouTube, music videos were an event — the crew breaks down the videos that changed everything, from late-80s cinema to the early-2000s clips that built hip-hop and R&B's visual language.

EP. 103

Sneakers: Inside the Revolution of Michael Jordan's Shoes

The crew dives deep into how a single Nike deal with Michael Jordan changed footwear, fashion, and culture forever — and what it meant to rock a pair of Jordans growing up in Detroit.
